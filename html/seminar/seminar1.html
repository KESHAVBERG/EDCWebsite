Introduction

“Thadai Athai Udai” a phenomenal entrepreneual talk was conducted on 21.10.2022 . We had presence of three eminent chief guest Mr.Sathya Pradeep,Mr . Balamurugan and Mr.Senthamil Selvan. The Seminar was inaugurated by Dr. C.Kumar. DEAN ALUMINI.

Mr.Sathya Pradeep, Founder and CEO of Furnify .Being the founder of Furnify, he was Down-toearth person, he engaged the audience with his words of wisdom.He interacted with the students regarding their interest to become and entrepreneurs.He also appreciated by each and everyone’s interest to become an entrepreneur at this young age. His talk encouraged students about the idea of entrepreneurship and its impact on society. His speech had left a powerful impact on the students.

Mr.Balamurugan SP, co-Founder of GUVI has given us a very inspirational talk on how to actually build a startup and ways to do it. He also said that he would not only support the students for their startup but actually guide them in many other ways. His guidance gave the students more motivation and strength.

Mr.Senthamail Arasan, A very enthusiastic speaker, told the students to initiate their startup and said that the government will support them financially. He also encourages students to ask him many queries and he answered them with meaningful answers. His advice did really make a huge remark on every student who attended the event.

With nearly 300 participants and with great ideas to begin a startup by great entrepreneurs, the team effort with the support of the Management, E-Cell Coordinator and Students, the talk was a success.
