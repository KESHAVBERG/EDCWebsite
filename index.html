Entrepreneurship development cell - Rajalakshmi Engineering College

The Entrepreneurship Development Cell of Rajalakshmi Engineering College is an initiative taken by the students in the spirit of shedding light on the long journey of bringing life to an idea. We intend to create a legacy that inspires others to dream more, do more, become more, and strive to work together, toward the beneficial and economic growth of our independent nation.

  • Mission
  • Vision
We aim to create awareness of business opportunities and potential. Motivate the students and faculty of Rajalakshmi Engineering College by inculcating the spirit of innovation-driven Entrepreneurship. And positively impact the nation as a whole.
The Entrepreneurship Development Cell strives to ignite an entrepreneurial drive amongst the students of Rajalakshmi Engineering College, by creating an apt environment to cater to the needs of aspiring magnates.
